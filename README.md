# Dorn's Fast Travel v0.1.0

Adds free PDA simulated fast travel through the zone using real pathing and ambushes. Fits **S.T.A.L.K.E.R. G.A.M.M.A.** naturally. Also adds a Sneaking skill. See **PDA > Guide > Addons > Dorn's Fast Travel**.

## Description

Avoid walking everywhere without instant teleporting all over the zone. This mod simulates a real journey on your PDA using path-finding and hostile scans. When your path approaches hostiles, you get ambushed and dropped short of your destination. Sneak to avoid encounters at the cost of more time, thirst, hunger, and sleep, with a short cooldown.

Sneaking improves with use: more sneak attempts, faster travel, better ambush avoidance, and safer spawn distance — but travel never becomes risk-free.

Instant fast travel to any base or stash remains available but costs twice base GAMMA (rare/late-game use). Most travel should be journeys between zone transitions with ambushes along the way.

*Highly opinionated — restrictions are not configurable. Feedback welcome; more knobs unlikely.*

Inspired by *damage_zedd*'s **[The Long Road Ahead](https://discord.com/channels/912320241713958912/1501468759020404736)** mod.

## Notes

- Does **NOT** rely on *Fair Fast Travel* (suppresses FFT options) or *Limited Fast Travel*.
